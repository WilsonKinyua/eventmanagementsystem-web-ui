Frequently Asked Questions

Below are many Frequently Asked Questions about event ms, but if all your questions aren't answered here, please contact us and we will do our best to answer them promptly.

What is event ms?

event ms is a Web-based software application that provides tools for event management, planning and registration solutions as well as for venue management and booking solutions.

How does event ms work?

Our software is entirely online, and so you would access it via a Web browser. Upon creating an account on our website, you can then log into your account via our login page and, once you are logged in, start using our software tools. We do not offer a version of our tool that you can download and install locally on your own computer.

So is there anything I need to download to use event ms?

Nope. Our application is completely Web-based, so there's nothing to install or download. All you need is a device with an up-to-date Web browser and a connection to the Internet.

What Web browsers are best for using event ms?

We recommend using a current version of either Google Chrome, Firefox or Safari. However, you can also use Microsoft Edge. Our software IS NOT backwards compatible with any version of Internet Explorer.

Can I access my event ms account on my tablet or smartphone?

Yes, you can. Our application is mobile-responsive so you can use it via the Web browser on your tablet or smartphone. We don't offer native apps, but our software is built to work great via the Web browser on all Apple and Android devices.

How can I create an account with event ms?

You can create a trial account to try event ms free for 14 days. After that, you will need to sign up for a paid subscription in order to continue to use our tools after the trial period.

If I sign up for a free trial, what am I committing to?

Absolutely nothing. We do not collect your credit card information, and you can close your account at any time during the trial without any further obligation.

How do your packages and pricing work?

For full details, visit the event management software pricing page; the venue management software pricing page; or the floorplans tool pricing page

Do you offer customer support?

Yes, most certainly. We provide daily email support as well as live chat support on weekdays (7:30am-4:00pm Mountain Time). For select enterprise accounts, we also provide phone support (M-F, 7:30am-4pm Mountain Time).

What happens if your system goes down?

From Q1 2013 through Q1 2017, we have had 99.9998644% uptime. To compare, that's on par with Facebook, Twitter, Netflix and Eventbrite during the same time period. But, like any Web-based system, if you cannot connect to the Internet or if we are down for a short period of time, you will not be able to access your data during that period. If that is a dealbreaker, you should not use event ms or, for that matter, any other Web-based system. Nuf said.

How is my account information protected?

Good question. We make every effort and use the latest technologies to maintain the security and confidentiality of our customers' data, including:

  • The most up-to-date firewall protection on the servers where your information is stored.
  • Daily remote backup of your account information.
  • Use of Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption/protection to ensure that the connection between your computer and our servers is secure.
  • Encryption of all account passwords (even we don't know your password).

In addition, we will keep confidential and never sell or otherwise share any personally identifying information in your account pertaining to your events, users, contacts and attendees. For more details, check out our Privacy Policy.

How long has event ms been in business?

We released our first software product for the events industry in 2007 (which we subsequently wrapped into event ms), and we launched event ms in the autumn of 2013.

Do I own the information in my account?

Generally speaking, yes, you own the information that you enter into your account (provided the information you enter is not already the property of another entity). Please consult our Terms & Conditions for more details.

Can I import my existing data into my event ms account?

Yes, for the most part. To simplify setting up your account, we have built tools to quickly import contacts into your event ms account (users, vendors, contractors, clients, etc.) as well as attendee information (via vCard or .csv files). You can do a one-time import of calendar information from most major electronic calendars (via an .ics file). And you can sync your event ms calendar with most other electronic calendars (like Google, Apple, Yahoo, etc.).

If you have spreadsheets or .csv files with custom event data, we offer white-glove importing services for manually porting your data into your account; contact us for details and pricing.

Can I connect my event ms account to other applications?

Yes, event ms connects to other applications and platforms (like Salesforce, Quickbooks, etc.), via Zapier.
